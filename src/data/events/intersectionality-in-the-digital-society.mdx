## Event Description

### What will it be about?

We work every day to make our digital society more inclusive for everyone. However, today’s approach often focuses on one perspective of inequality at a time.

For example, we might assume that users with disabilities are “only” disabled—that they are still digitally competent, financially stable, with a fixed address, reliable internet access, support by their families, and so on.

### In this session, we will explore …

… intersecting forms of marginalisation, the limitations of current approaches to inclusion, and what we need to consider to build a more inclusive future from an intersectional lens 😊

### Who is this for?
This session is for anyone involved in or interested in digital inclusion, including designers, developers, product owners, leaders, etc.

While accessibility is a part of the discussion, no technical knowledge is required to follow the session 👌

**Join the session to foster a stronger digital inclusion culture in your team 🌱**
