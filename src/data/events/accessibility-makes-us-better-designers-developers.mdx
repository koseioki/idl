## Takeaways

* Accessibility helps not only people with disabilities, but everyone.

* Accessibility makes design choices faster, as non-accessible designs often fail in the development or testing phase.

* Accessibility and aesthetics can coexist if integrated from the beginning.

* Design for real people—guidelines are there to help you achieve that, not the other way around.
